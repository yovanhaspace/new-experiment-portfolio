𓂀 The Codex

For over two decades, Dr. Yovanna Pineda has traced how machines, money, and people shaped modern Argentina and Latin America. Her work spans economic history, the history of technology and science, documentary film, and digital humanities — from harvesters on the Pampas to virtual-reality reconstructions of the Middle Passage.
